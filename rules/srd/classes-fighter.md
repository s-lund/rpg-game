# Fighter (level 1 excerpt)

**Source:** Pathfinder Player Core, Class — Fighter (ORC). See Archives of Nethys Fighter class entry.

## Key ability

Strength **or** Dexterity (player assigns via standard array).

## Hit Points

10 + Constitution modifier at 1st level (and each level thereafter).

## Skills (1st level)

- Trained in **Acrobatics** or **Athletics** (player picks one)
- Trained in a number of additional skills equal to **3 + Intelligence modifier**

## Attacks (1st level)

- Expert in simple weapons
- Expert in martial weapons
- Expert in unarmed attacks

## Defenses

- Trained in all armor
- M2 uses chain mail + shield defaults (see `m2-subset.json`)
