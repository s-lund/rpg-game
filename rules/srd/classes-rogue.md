# Rogue (level 1 excerpt)

**Source:** Pathfinder Player Core, Class — Rogue (ORC). See Archives of Nethys Rogue class entry.

## Key ability

Dexterity (primary for M2 tactical derivation).

## Hit Points

8 + Constitution modifier at 1st level (and each level thereafter).

## Skills (1st level)

- Trained in **Stealth** (mandatory)
- Trained in a number of additional skills equal to **7 + Intelligence modifier**

## Sneak Attack (1st level)

When you Strike a flat-footed creature with an agile or finesse melee weapon, you deal an extra **1d6** precision damage.

M2 maps flanking to flat-footed for sneak attack eligibility (combat simplification carried from M1).

## Attacks (1st level)

- Trained in simple weapons
- Trained in rapier, sap, shortbow, shortsword
- M2 uses shortsword defaults (see `m2-subset.json`)
