# Wizard (level 1 excerpt)

**Source:** Pathfinder Player Core, Class — Wizard (ORC).

## Key ability

Intelligence.

## Hit Points

6 + Constitution modifier at 1st level.

## Skills (1st level)

Trained in a number of additional skills equal to **2 + Intelligence modifier**.

## Spells (M7 subset)

- **Ray of Frost** cantrip (2 actions, ranged spell attack, 2d4 cold) — see `spell-ray-of-frost.md`
