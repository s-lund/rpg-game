# Heal (spell 1)

**Source:** Pathfinder Player Core (ORC).

- **Rank:** 1 — a leveled spell. From M9 on it is prepared into and consumes rank-1 spell slots (regular cleric slots and divine font slots; see `spell-slots.md`, `cleric-divine-font.md`).
- **Cast:** 1 to 3 actions
- **M7+ uses:** 2 actions — range 30 feet (6 tiles), 1d8+8 HP restored to a willing living creature (including self). Restores downed allies at 0 HP back to consciousness.
- **Undead:** deals positive (vitality) damage with a basic Fortitude save — **deferred to M17 bestiary** (no undead-trait creatures exist yet; M9 ships basic saves via Breathe Fire)

Heightened (+1): healing increases by 1d8; 2-action flat bonus increases by 8.
