# Ray of Frost (cantrip)

**Source:** Pathfinder Player Core (ORC).

- **Cast:** 2 actions (somatic, verbal)
- **Range:** 120 feet (M7 maps to 6 tiles)
- **Target:** 1 creature
- **Attack:** spell attack roll vs AC
- **Damage:** 2d4 cold (Player Core remaster; no ability modifier to damage)
- **Cantrip:** cast at will; does not consume spell slots

M7 implements attack roll and damage only; critical success speed penalty deferred.
