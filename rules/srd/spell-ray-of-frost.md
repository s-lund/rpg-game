# Ray of Frost (cantrip)

**Source:** Pathfinder Player Core (ORC). Trait line verified 2026-06-12 via Archives of Nethys (see below).

- **Cast:** 2 actions (somatic, verbal)
- **Traits:** attack, cantrip, cold, **concentrate**, **manipulate**
- **Range:** 120 feet (M7 maps to 6 tiles)
- **Target:** 1 creature
- **Attack:** spell attack roll vs AC
- **Damage:** 2d4 cold (Player Core remaster; no ability modifier to damage)
- **Cantrip:** cast at will; does not consume spell slots

M7 implements attack roll and damage only; critical success speed penalty deferred.

## Trait line (M12, load-bearing for Reactive Strike)

The legacy printing casts with **somatic and verbal** components. Under the
remaster transition, a somatic component maps to the **manipulate** trait and a
verbal component to **concentrate**; the remaster Player Core's equivalent cold
cantrip ([Frostbite — Archives of Nethys](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=1539),
which AoN redirects "Ray of Frost" to) carries the traits **cantrip, cold,
concentrate, manipulate** explicitly. Either way: casting this spell is a
manipulate action — it provokes Reactive Strike when cast within a reactor's
reach, and a critical hit from that reaction disrupts the cast
(`reactive-strike.md`, M12 scope). It is also a ranged (spell) attack, which is
itself a RAW trigger; the two reasons overlap and still cost the reactor only
its one reaction.
